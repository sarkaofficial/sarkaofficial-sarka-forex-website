About Sarka Forex

Sarka Forex is Africa’s #1 AI-powered trading company, built to empower a new generation of traders and investors. We combine world-class artificial intelligence, automation, and a strong community to help our users achieve consistent results and financial freedom.

Our Mission

To make advanced trading, financial knowledge, and wealth-building tools accessible to every African, regardless of background.

Our Vision

Building Africa’s most trusted, impactful, and automated trading platform — where every member can grow, earn, and thrive with the support of cutting-edge technology and a powerful community.

Why "Ghost Mode"?

Sarka Forex is designed as a community movement and platform—not about any single founder. Our focus is on scalable technology, AI, and user results. We remain in “ghost mode” so all value is for the people.
